Bachelor of Arts (B.A) and Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) programs are regular programs of the Liberal Arts program in MIT. This is a two-semester four years study program, offering six different majors such as Business Studies, Computer Studies, English, Music, Religion Studies and Social Studies and the study covers 123 credits in total

WHY CHOOSE US ?

Why Choose MIT'S Bachelors' Degree?

  • Quality - The program works with both International and local experience lecturers, providing an interactive learning environment for whole-person development.
  • Convenience - All courses are offered in the evening (5:00-8:00pm) for working students.
  • Time to Degree - 4 years
  • Affordable - Kyats 2,729,000 in total (approximately 760,000 per year). (Note: This fee is only for online program and subject to change. Students are allowed to pay with installment.)

ABOUT US

Myanmar Institute of Theology (MIT) is a Protestant Christian Baptist seminary in Insein Township, Yangon, Myanmar (Burma). Judson Research Center, Peace Studies Center, and Gender Studies Center are parts of Myanmar Institute of Theology. MIT's library has over 30,000 usable volumes and more than 30 periodical subscriptions for students and faculty.
